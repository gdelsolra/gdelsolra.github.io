The App Experience
From the moment users sign in, Appetite Lab guides them through a beautiful, intuitive journey. The app features four core sections:
Dashboard
Users see their monthly impact at a glance: fast food spending, grocery costs, and potential savings. The dashboard tracks their favorite orders and shows how cooking at home translates to real savings.
Smart Inventory
Track ingredients with automatic expiry alerts. The system shows what's expiring soon, what's fresh, and provides an overview of the entire pantry. Never waste food again.
Recipe Intelligence
Get matched with recipes based on ingredients you already own and flavors you love. Each recipe shows the match percentage, what you have, what you need, and exactly how much you'll save versus ordering out.
Personalized Onboarding
A quick 2-minute quiz learns cuisine preferences, dietary restrictions, and cooking time availability. The app tailors all recommendations to match each user's unique tastes and lifestyle.
