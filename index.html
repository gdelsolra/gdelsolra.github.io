About

This is my final-term ePortfolio. I’m showcasing enhancements to my Appetite App: a seamless experience that connects Pantry → Calendar → Recipes → Groceries, with an optional goals connector (MacroFactor‑style).

Artifacts

  • Software Design & UX: Pantry, Calendar planning, and recipe flows.
  • Algorithms & Data Structures: Pantry‑aware recipe scoring (coverage, missing cost, time fit, goals).
  • Databases: Normalized SQLite schema with indexing and JSON export.
